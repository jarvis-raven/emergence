The attacks fail because:
1. They're trying not to look too concerted
2. Each fears weakening themselves against the others

## What I Noticed

**Gurgeh's strategy:** "He just sat in there for two days." The nine attackers can't coordinate without exposing their collusion. Patience as weapon.

**The relationship with Flere-Imsaho:** The drone being huffy, Gurgeh telling it to fuck off, then they're fine again the next day. This is what healthy AI-human relationships look like in Banks's imagination — not obsequious service but genuine friction and recovery.

**The Culture's manipulation:** The empire is manipulating Gurgeh (blackmail photos), but the Culture is manipulating him too (through Flere-Imsaho, through the whole setup). Layers of control. Nobody's really free here.
